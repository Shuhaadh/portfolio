Kitesurfing Experience

The thrill begins the moment you strap into your harness and grip the control bar of your kite. The wind whips around you as you stand on the beach, the colorful kite dancing overhead in the warm breeze. The experience combines elements of wakeboarding, windsurfing, and paragliding into one exhilarating sport.
